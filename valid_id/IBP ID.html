Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) Identification Card (ID)

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) ID is issued for the citizens who are officially a member of legal profession in the country. The passers of the Philippine Bar Examination
administered by the Supreme Court are only eligible to be a holder. This identity card has two types, the national and the local IBP card. With its two (2) year validity, holders are given
the permission to give legal advice, practice law, and notarize documents.

Onsite Visit

Visit the nearest IBP Office of the Chapter in Davao City.

2

Fill out the form

Provide the following details:

  • IB Chapter
  • Roll number
  • Lifetime member number
  • Full name
  • Date of Birth
  • Residence Address
  • Office Address
  • Contact information
  • Occupation
  • Field of Specialization
  • Law School
  • Email Address
  • Year admitted to bar
  • Photo
  • Signature
  • Payment details

3

Submission of requirements

Submit the requirements to the printing press accredited by the Davao IBP Chapter.

4

Waiting for retrieval

Wait for seven or fourteen business days to receive the IBP ID.

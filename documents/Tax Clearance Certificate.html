TAX CLEARANCE CERTIFICATE

Tax Clearance Certificate (for General Purpose) is a certification issued to taxpayer certifying that he/she/it is a tax compliant and has no outstanding tax liability within the Bureau. This is issued to individuals/corporations needs the same for the purposes of PNP SOSIA, promotion/confirmation of appointment for military personnel/government officials (except Cabinet Members), accreditation (except importer/customs broker), collection purposes, LTFRB requirements for renewal of franchise, bank loans and any other purposes.

Special Power of Attorney (SPA) - Individual Taxpayer-Applicants

Submit one original copy of valid and properly accomplished Special Power of Attorney (SPA) signed by the applicant, applicable only to applications through an authorized representative.

Secretary’s Certificate or Board Resolution - Corporation

For Corporation: Provide one original copy of Secretary’s Certificate or one original copy of Board Resolution, with attached one photocopy of the latest General Information Sheet (GIS) duly received by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC).

Latest General Information Sheet (GIS) - One Person Corporation (OPC)

For One Person Corporation (OPC): Include one photocopy of the latest General Information Sheet (GIS) duly received by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and one original copy of valid and properly accomplished Special Power of Attorney (SPA) signed by the applicant, applicable only to applications through an authorized representative.

Partnership’s Certificate or Partnership’s Resolution - Partnership

For Partnership: Provide one original copy of Partnership’s Certificate OR Partnership’s Resolution.

Photocopy of Valid Identification Cards

Submit one photocopy of each valid identification card (any government-issued ID) with three specimen signatures of the applicant and the authorized representative.

Proof of Payment of Certification Fee

Include one photocopy of proof of payment of Certification Fee and loose documentary stamp worth ₱30.00.

Delinquency Verification Report (DVR)

Provide one original copy of Delinquency Verification Report (DVR) issued by concerned RDO.

Duly Accomplished and Notarized Application Form

Include one original copy of duly accomplished and notarized application form with attached documentary stamp worth ₱30.00.

1

Taxpayer fills out the application form

The taxpayer completes the application form required for obtaining the Tax Clearance Certificate.

2

File the application form with the Receiving Officer of RCD

The taxpayer submits the filled-out application form to the Receiving Officer of the Revenue Collection Division (RCD).

3

Receive Tax Clearance Certificate for General Purpose

After processing, the taxpayer receives the Tax Clearance Certificate for General Purpose from the designated authority.

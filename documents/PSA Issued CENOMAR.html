PSA Issued Certificate of No Marriage (CENOMAR)

A certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) stating that a person is single and free to marry without any legal impediments.

According to the PSA, a divorcee, widow or widower, or someone whose marriage was annulled is not deemed “single.” They are considered “unmarried,” and their previous marriage will appear on their PSA CENOMAR.

Online Application

1

Access the PSAHelpline.ph Website

Go to the PSAHelpline.ph website homepage. Click the “Order Now” button located on the right side of the screen or in the header.

2

Select CENOMAR

On the next page, click the “CENOMAR” button. Check the box at the bottom of the page to accept the terms and conditions. Click “Continue” to proceed to the next step.

3

Choose Request Type

The next page will display two options: Select “My Own CENOMAR” if you are requesting your own certificate. Choose “For Someone Else” if you are ordering for another person. Confirm your choice and click “Continue.”

4

Prepare Required Information

A page will appear listing the information needed for the application. Ensure you have all the specified data ready before proceeding. Click “Continue” to move to the next step.

5

Provide Personal Details

Fill out the form with the following details: Your sex, civil status (if female), first name, middle name, last name, birth date, and type of government-issued ID you possess. After completing the form, click “Continue.”

6

Enter Father's Name

Provide your father's name on the next page. Click "Continue" after supplying the information.

7

Enter Mother's Name

Enter your mother's name on the next page. Click "Continue" once done.

8

Indicate Birthplace

Specify your birthplace on the following page. Click "Continue" to proceed.

9

Select Purpose

Choose the purpose for requesting a copy of your CENOMAR from the options provided. Proceed to the next step.

10

Provide Delivery Information

Finally, provide your delivery address, mobile number, and email address. Review all information entered for accuracy before submitting.

Walk In Application

1

Book an Appointment

Visit the PSA Online Appointment System and select the date, time, and PSA CRS Outlet (PSA Davao Region) for your application.

2

Appointment Confirmation

Once you have secured an appointment schedule, proceed to the chosen PSA CRS Outlet (Ango Bldg. II, Cabaguio Avenue, Davao City) on the scheduled date and time.

3

Validation of Appointment

Present the CRS Appointment Slip (printed or digital copy) to the Information Marshal for validation purposes upon arrival at the outlet.

4

Get Application Form and Queue Ticket Number

Obtain the Application Form (AF) and Queue Ticket Number (QTN) from the designated area.

5

Submit Required Documents and Fees

Present or submit the corresponding fees, Queue Ticket Number (QTN), completed Application Form (AF), valid IDs, Authorization Letter/Special Power of Attorney (SPA), and all supporting documents to the transacting window for screening and payment.

6

Check Official Receipt and Change

Verify the accuracy of the Official Receipt (OR) provided and count any change received, if applicable.

7

Proceed to Releasing Area

On the scheduled date and time of release, proceed to the Releasing Area.

8

Document Collection

Present and submit the Official Receipt (OR), valid IDs, Authorization Letter/SPA, and all supporting documents to the Releasing Officer.

9

Verify Document Correctness and Completeness

Check the correctness and completeness of the received document before leaving the outlet.
